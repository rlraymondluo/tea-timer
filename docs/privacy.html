TL;DR: Tea Timer doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We respect your privacy completely.

Overview

Tea Timer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Data Collection

We collect ZERO data.

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No tracking cookies
  • No device identifiers
  • No location data
  • No network activity monitoring

Local Data Storage

Tea Timer stores data locally on your device only:

  • Custom Tea Profiles: Saved to your device's local storage (UserDefaults)
  • App Preferences: Settings like favorite teas, stored locally
  • Timer State: Current brewing session, stored temporarily in memory

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any servers or cloud services (unless you use iCloud backup, which is managed by Apple, not us).

Third-Party Services

Tea Timer does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks. There are:

  • No advertisements
  • No social media integrations
  • No analytics tools
  • No crash reporting services

Permissions

Tea Timer may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when brewing rounds are complete (optional, can be denied)

That's it! We don't need access to your contacts, camera, location, or anything else.

Internet Connection

Tea Timer works entirely offline. No internet connection is required or used.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since all data stays on your device and we don't collect any data, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device locked with a passcode
  • Using iOS's built-in security features
  • Enabling iCloud backup if you want to backup your custom teas

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version in the app and update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to:

  • Access
  • Delete
  • Export
  • Modify

All your data is already under your control on your device.

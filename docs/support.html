Your guide to perfect tea brewing

Getting Started

  1. Choose Your Tea: Select from 5 pre-configured teas or create your own
  2. Start Brewing: Tap the play button to begin the timer
  3. Multiple Rounds: The app automatically progresses through each steeping round
  4. Unlimited Steeps: Continue brewing beyond the default rounds

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom tea?

1. Scroll to the bottom of the tea selection screen
2. Tap "Create Custom Tea"
3. Enter your tea name, rounds, and timing
4. Tap "Save" - your custom tea will appear in the list

Can I delete a custom tea?

Yes! Long-press (tap and hold) on any custom tea card, then select "Delete" from the menu.

What are the round indicators?

The dots below the timer show your current round. Tap any dot to jump to that round!

How do unlimited steeps work?

After completing all pre-defined rounds, the app automatically adds additional rounds with increased steep times. You can steep indefinitely!

Can I adjust the timer during brewing?

Yes! Use the ¬± buttons to quickly add or remove time while brewing.

Does the app work offline?

Absolutely! Tea Timer works completely offline with no internet connection required.

Do you collect my data?

No! We collect zero data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

Why use Fahrenheit instead of Celsius?

Version 1.0 uses Fahrenheit. Celsius support is planned for a future update!

Tea Brewing Tips

  • Water Temperature: Follow the recommended temps for best flavor
  • First Steep: Often shorter to "rinse" the leaves
  • Subsequent Steeps: Gradually increase time for continued flavor
  • Experiment: Create custom profiles to match your taste
